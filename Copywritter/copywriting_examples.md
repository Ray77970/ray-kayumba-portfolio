# Multilingual Copywriting Skill: Practical Examples

## Example 1: A SaaS Product (Project Management Tool)

**Product**: Asana alternative, focus on small teams
**Goal**: Get trial signups from small business owners
**Target Audience**: 
- EN: US/UK small business owners, 28-45, stressed by complexity
- DE: German Mittelstand, 35-50, value precision & ROI
- FR: French entrepreneurs, 30-50, value elegance & efficiency

---

### 🇬🇧 ENGLISH VERSION

**Headline**: 
"Stop losing projects to email chaos."

**Subheadline**: 
"See every deadline at a glance. Your team stays synced. You stay sane."

**Opening Hook**:
"Your inbox is a graveyard of half-finished projects. Deadlines slip. Teammates repeat work. You're drowning in back-and-forths."

**Main Body** (3 benefits):
1. **One place for everything**: No more hunting through Slack, email, and spreadsheets. Your entire project lives here.
2. **Everyone sees the same thing**: No more "I didn't know that was due today." Deadlines are visible, updated in real-time.
3. **Less time managing, more time doing**: Your team spends 12 hours/week less on status updates. That's your time back.

**Social Proof**:
"1,200+ small teams trust us. Average user saves 2.5 hours per week."

**CTA Button**: 
"Start my free 14-day trial"

**Why this works for English**:
- ✓ Opens with pain point (emotional hook)
- ✓ "You/your" language (customer-centric)
- ✓ Specific metric (2.5 hours = concrete benefit)
- ✓ Action-driven CTA with benefit ("start" + "free")
- ✓ Conversational tone ("You're drowning")

---

### 🇩🇪 GERMAN VERSION

**Headline**: 
"Sichere Projektplanung ohne Planungschaos."

**Subheadline**: 
"Alle Fristen übersichtlich. Ihr Team arbeitet synchron. Sie sparen Zeit."

**Opening Hook**:
"In den meisten Small-Business-Teams laufen Projektinformationen über mindestens vier Kanäle: E-Mail, Slack, Excel, Papier. Das führt zu Verzögerungen, Doppelarbeit und verpassten Terminen."

**Main Body** (Exactly 3 benefits with proof):
1. **Vollständige Transparenz**: Alle Fristen, Zuständigkeiten und Abhängigkeiten an einer Stelle. Die Suchzeit nach Informationen sinkt nachweislich um 67%.
2. **Automatische Synchronisation**: Ihre Teams aktualisieren nicht mehr manuell Status-Updates. Das spart durchschnittlich 2,5 Stunden pro Mitarbeiter pro Woche.
3. **Messbares Projekt-Tracking**: Echte Daten statt Vermutungen. Sie sehen exakt, welche Projekte im Plan sind und wo Verzögerungen drohen.

**Mehrwert-Statement** (unique to German approach):
"Anders als Standard-Tools bietet [Produkt] eine Schnittstelle speziell für deutschsprachige Teams. Zeittracking, Kostenplanung und Compliance-Anforderungen sind vorgebaut—nicht für später."

**Social Proof** (German style = data-heavy):
"✓ 1.200+ Mittelstand-Unternehmen verlassen sich auf uns.
✓ 94% erreichen ihre Deadlines zuverlässiger.
✓ TÜV-zertifizierte Datensicherheit (Deutschland/EU)."

**CTA Button**: 
"Kostenlose Demo anfordern (unverbindlich)"

**Why this works for German**:
- ✓ Opens with concrete problem (logic-first)
- ✓ Specific metrics for each benefit (67%, 2.5 hours, 94%)
- ✓ Mehrwert articulated (what's different about THIS tool)
- ✓ Trust signals: TÜV certification, EU compliance
- ✓ Formal tone ("Ihr Team arbeitet", not "du fragst")
- ✓ CTA is info-first ("Demo") not immediate sale
- ✓ "unverbindlich" (no-pressure) messaging

### 🇫🇷 FRENCH VERSION

**Headline**: 
"La maîtrise de vos projets. L'art de la collaboration."

**Subheadline**: 
"Donnez à votre équipe l'espace pour créer. Gardez le contrôle des délais."

**Opening Hook**:
"Les grandes entreprises ont des directeurs de projet. Les petites équipes doivent se débrouiller seules. Emails dispersés, Slack fragmenté, spreadsheets obsolètes. C'est inefficace. C'est frustrant."

**Main Body** (3 benefits, aesthetically framed):
1. **Clarté et élégance opérationnelle**: Un seul espace, intuitif et raffiné. Vos équipes retrouvent naturellement ce qu'elles cherchent, sans formation complexe.
2. **Synchronisation harmonieuse**: Chacun travaille à son rythme, mais reste connecté. Les mises à jour se propagent instantanément. L'harmonie règne.
3. **Transparence intelligente**: Voyez l'état réel de vos projets, en un coup d'œil. Anticipez les retards. Décidez avec confiance.

**Heritage/Quality Messaging**:
"Conçu en France, pour les équipes qui valorisent la qualité, la clarté et l'efficacité. Pas de complexité inutile. Juste ce qui marche."

**Social Proof** (French style = prestige):
"Approuvé par 300+ entreprises créatives et innovantes. Les leaders du secteur nous font confiance."

**CTA Button**: 
"Découvrez votre démo personnalisée"

**Why this works for French**:
- ✓ Headline emphasizes artistry ("l'art de la collaboration")
- ✓ Elegant descriptions ("harmonieuse", "raffiné", "clarté")
- ✓ Heritage mention ("Conçu en France")
- ✓ Quality-focused ("les leaders du secteur")
- ✓ Tone is sophisticated, not pushy
- ✓ CTA is aspirational ("Découvrez", not "buy now")

## Example 2: Email Newsletter Signup (Technical Blog)

**Product**: Weekly technical insights newsletter
**Goal**: Grow subscriber base among software developers
**Target**: Developers in DACH region (multi-language)

---

### 🇬🇧 ENGLISH EMAIL

**Subject**: "Learn the one thing that makes fast APIs 3x faster"

**Body**:
"Hi [Name],

Ever deployed an API that was slower than your competitors'?

[Company] just analyzed 500+ production APIs. We found one optimization pattern that appears in 94% of high-performance systems.

It's not what you'd expect.

We're sharing exactly what it is (with working code) in this week's newsletter.

[BUTTON: Get the deep dive]

No spam, just one great article every Thursday.

—[Author]"

**Why this works**:
- Curiosity gap ("not what you'd expect")
- Specific metric (500+ APIs, 94%)
- Low friction ("one article, weekly")

---

### 🇩🇪 GERMAN EMAIL

**Subject**: "API-Performance: 1 Pattern, 3x schneller — mit Beweis"

**Body**:
"Hallo [Name],

94% aller hochperformanten APIs folgen einem bestimmten Optimierungsmuster. Die übrigen 6% scheitern regelmäßig unter Last.

Unsere Analyse von 500+ Produktions-APIs zeigt: Dieses Muster ist nicht optional. Es ist kritisch.

Diese Woche veröffentlichen wir die exakten Implementierungsschritte, eine vollständige Code-Beispiel und drei häufige Fehler, die Sie vermeiden sollten.

[BUTTON: Artikel kostenfrei lesen]

Newsletter: Ein Artikel, jede Woche Donnerstag. Abmeldung jederzeit möglich.

—[Author]"

**Why this works**:
- Opens with data (94%, 6%)
- States clear unique value ("exakten Implementierungsschritte")
- Formal tone ("Hallo" not "Hey")
- Transparency ("Abmeldung jederzeit" = builds trust)

---

### 🇫🇷 FRENCH EMAIL

**Subject**: "L'art d'optimiser une API : le secret des meilleures"

**Body**:
"Bonjour [Name],

Les équipes les plus talentueuses partagent une pratique commune : une seule optimisation qui transforme les APIs lentes en systèmes fluides.

Nous l'avons documentée en analysant 500+ architectures en production.

Cette semaine, nous vous montrons exactement ce que c'est. Avec le code. Et les pièges à éviter.

[BUTTON: Lire l'article exclusif]

Un email, chaque jeudi. Pensé pour ceux qui valorisent la qualité technique.

—[Author]"

**Why this works**:
- Frame as "art" (élégance)
- "Les équipes les plus talentueuses" (prestige)
- "pensé pour ceux qui valorisent" (quality-focused, exclusive)

## The 12x12x12 Revision Checklist (German-Inspired)

After publishing your German copy, schedule:

**Day 12:**
- [ ] Google has indexed it
- [ ] Check initial performance (views, clicks)
- [ ] Review for obvious typos/errors
- [ ] Update with early data (e.g., "1,200+ teams" if you started at 800)

**Week 12:**
- [ ] Analyze: Which sections got most clicks/shares?
- [ ] Identify weak spots (low engagement areas)
- [ ] Refresh with new examples or case studies
- [ ] Update certifications/awards if applicable

**Month 12:**
- [ ] Major refresh with new trends/research
- [ ] Check if any claims are outdated
- [ ] Add new sections based on FAQ/customer feedback
- [ ] Re-optimize tone if brand voice has evolved

## Quick Decision Tree: Which Approach?

```
START
  ↓
Is audience primarily English-speaking (US/UK/Australia)?
  YES → Use ENGLISH module (emotional, benefit-driven)
  NO → Continue
  ↓
Is audience German/Austrian/Swiss (DACH region)?
  YES → Use GERMAN module (goal-first, proof-driven, 12x12x12)
  NO → Continue
  ↓
Is audience French-speaking?
  YES → Use FRENCH module (elegant, quality-focused)
  NO → Use language-agnostic UNIVERSAL module
```

## Implementation Checklist for Your Agent

When using this skill, ensure your agent:

1. **Detects language** from user request
2. **Routes to correct module** (EN/DE/FR)
3. **Gathers context** (goal, audience, Mehrwert)
4. **Writes copy** following language-specific rules
5. **Provides alternatives** (headlines, CTAs)
6. **Includes revision strategy**:
   - English: A/B testing strategy
   - German: 12x12x12 schedule
   - French: Quality refinement notes

7. **Annotates choices** with cultural/linguistic reasoning
8. **Delivers quality checklist** specific to that language

## Summary: What Makes This Multilingual

| Aspect | English | German | French |
|--------|---------|--------|---------|
| **Lead with** | Emotion + benefit | Goal + data | Elegance + heritage |
| **Structure** | Hook → Benefits → CTA | Goal → Mehrwert → Structure → CTA | Aesthetics → Value → Refinement |
| **Tone** | Casual, direct, empathetic | Formal, expert-driven, precise | Elegant, cultivated, intellectual |
| **Evidence** | Testimonials, user counts | Certifications, metrics, data | Awards, prestige, heritage |
| **CTA** | Action-oriented ("Get started") | Info-first ("Mehr erfahren") | Aspirational ("Découvrez") |
| **Post-Publication** | A/B test variants | 12x12x12 revision cycle | Quality refinement |
